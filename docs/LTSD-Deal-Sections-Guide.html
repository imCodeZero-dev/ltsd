🏷 Deal Types

⚡ Lightning Deals

Time-limited flash sales from Amazon (4–12 hours). Limited inventory with live % claimed tracking and countdown timer. Sourced via Keepa's /lightningdeal endpoint.

Duration
4–12 hours
Live Data
% Claimed + Timer
Sync
3x daily

📈 Price Drop Deals

Products currently 10%+ below their Keepa 90-day average price. Verified through historical price data — not inflated "original" prices. Sourced via Keepa's /deal + /product endpoints.

Detection compares the current price against the weighted 90-day average, not the absolute 90-day low. This means a product at $82 with an average of $100 qualifies as a Price Drop even if it briefly hit $75 during a past sale event. The average-based approach catches more genuine deals without being thrown off by one-time spikes.

Detection
90-day avg comparison
Min Drop
10% below avg
Sync
3x daily

⏰ Limited Time Deals

Products on sale with 10%+ discount that don't qualify as Price Drops (less than 10% below their 90-day average, or no average data available). Includes best sellers, category promotions, and general discounts. Same Keepa source endpoints as Price Drops.

Min Discount
10% off
Duration
Days to weeks
Sync
3x daily
